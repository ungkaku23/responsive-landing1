ABOUT US

By grace of almighty ALLAH, Our company TOTAL EQUIPMENT & MACHINERY BD. is a leading Trading firm.We represent the interest of our foreign Manufacturers and Suppliers in our local market. We deal Turnkey Projects & Industrial Machinery and heavy duty Constructions Equipment.

TEAM BD. also deal in the Public Sectors through Participating in their International tenders for Power Plant, Railway, Road Division etc. In the Private Sectors, we sell Industrial Machinery & heavy duty Equipment through offering quotations to local buyers from our foreign Manufacturers and Suppliers.

TEAM BD. is able to offer Quality Services responding to the needs of users by supplying required equipment, spares and materials for the smooth operation, repair and maintenance of different Industrial plants in Bangladesh.Nature of Business: Industrial Machinery & Heavy Duty Equipment.
